Thesis

A Comparative Analysis of Quantized and Non-Quantized BERT Model Performance for the Low-Resource Tagalog Language through Binary Text Classification (2022-2024)

In our study, we employ Google's powerful transformer model BERT for binary sentiment analysis, a crucial task in natural language processing. Leveraging state-of-the-art machine learning techniques, we apply quantization to the transformer model. This approach aims to optimize the model by significantly reducing hardware requirements without compromising its capability in accurately classifying text sentiment. Our research highlights the efficacy of quantization in enhancing the efficiency and practicality of deep learning models for real-world applications.
